About Us

Welcome to the City of Lights!!

Las Vegas is known to everyone as a city of bright lights and much toursim, unsuprisingly an attraction to Las Vegas is the number of Casinos that occupy the city. People from all over the world come to this city for a good time, and possibly make some money. Las Vegas is not as known city for history, however, Sin City has quite a bizarre history filled with Paradoxes and funny business galore. The city was first established after the Mexican-American War when General John C Fremont and his friend Kit Carson explored the area on a pioneering trip. By the mid 1850s, Las Vegas would be a stop for Brigham Young and the Mormon followers, funny as Vegas is not seen as a city of religion. The Nevada Silver Industry would bring people and the railroad to Vegas, which now put Las Vegas on the map. It was not until 1910 when the first Casino was established for the silver miners in the city. During the 1920s, Las Vegas did not grow as compared to places like Los Angeles but in the next decade Las Vegas grew dramatically. Thanks to the creation of the Hoover Dam, workers would make Las Vegas home for much of the decade as it was cheaper compared to that of the coasts. In the 1940s Las Vegas would begin its transition into that of a tourist hub. New Casinos were being built and the building of new military airbases brought more families to the Las Vegas area, Thus is the reason why McCarran International Airport, the 9th busiest Airport in the United States. WIth new Casinos brought the attention of Mobsters and Gangsters. In the 1950s, Jewish mob and the Italian Mafia moved a lot of their "operations" to the Sin City. Although there was an increase in crime, tourism in Sin city further boomed.
